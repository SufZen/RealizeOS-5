# SaaS Venture — Strategic Goal

**Mission**: Build and scale a B2B SaaS product that reaches $1M ARR within
18 months through product-led growth, community-driven adoption, and
exceptional developer experience.

## Current Quarter Focus
- Ship v2.0 with multi-tenant and API key management
- Reach 500 active users / 50 paid customers
- Establish publishing cadence: 3 blog posts + 1 case study per month
- Achieve <2 hour median first-response for support tickets

## Success Metrics
- Monthly active users: 500 target
- Conversion rate (free → paid): >5%
- Monthly churn: <3%
- NPS: >50
