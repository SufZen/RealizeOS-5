# Buying Property in Spain — Process Guide

## Overview

The Spanish property buying process typically takes 6-12 weeks from offer acceptance. The process involves a notario for the final deed and registration at the Registro de la Propiedad.

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Preparation
- Obtain NIE (Número de Identidad de Extranjero) — required for all transactions
- Open a Spanish bank account
- Engage a lawyer (abogado) for due diligence
- Obtain mortgage pre-approval if financing (typically 60-70% LTV for non-residents)

### 2. Reserva (Reservation)
- Small holding deposit (EUR 3,000-6,000) to take property off market
- Usually refundable within a short window (5-10 days)
- Not always used — can go directly to arras

### 3. Contrato de Arras (Deposit Contract)
- Binding agreement — typically Arras Penitenciales
- Deposit: 10% of purchase price
- If buyer withdraws: loses deposit
- If seller withdraws: must return double the deposit
- Sets completion deadline (usually 30-90 days)

### 4. Due Diligence
- **Nota Simple** (Registro de la Propiedad) — ownership, charges, liens
- **Catastro** reference and boundaries
- **IBI receipts** — confirm taxes are paid
- **Community fees** — check for outstanding payments
- **Building license** and first occupation license
- **Certificado Energético** — energy certificate (mandatory)
- **Cédula de Habitabilidad** — habitation certificate (some regions)

### 5. Escritura Pública (Public Deed)
- Signed before a notario
- Balance paid (bank transfer or bank check)
- Taxes paid on or shortly after signing
- Keys handed over
- Notario sends for registration at Registro de la Propiedad

### 6. Post-Completion
- Register at Registro de la Propiedad (handled by gestoría or lawyer)
- Pay ITP or IVA (if not already paid)
- Change utility contracts (agua, luz, gas)
- Register with community of owners
- Update catastro records

## Costs for Buyer

| Cost | Amount |
|---|---|
| ITP (resale) | 6-10% (varies by region) |
| IVA + AJD (new build) | 10% IVA + 0.5-1.5% AJD |
| Notary fees | EUR 600-1,200 |
| Registry fees | EUR 400-700 |
| Legal fees | EUR 1,500-3,000 |
| Gestoría fees | EUR 300-600 |
| Agency commission | 3-5% (usually paid by seller) |

*Note: ITP rates vary significantly by autonomous community. Verify current regional rates.*
