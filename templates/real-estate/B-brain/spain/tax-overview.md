# Spanish Property Taxes — Overview

## Transaction Taxes (One-Time)

### Resale Properties
- **ITP (Impuesto de Transmisiones Patrimoniales):** 6-10% of purchase price
  - Varies by autonomous community (e.g., Andalucía 7%, Cataluña 10%, Madrid 6%)
  - Reduced rates may apply for young buyers, large families, social housing

### New Build Properties (from Developer)
- **IVA:** 10% of purchase price (4% for social housing)
- **AJD (Actos Jurídicos Documentados):** 0.5-1.5% (varies by region)

## Annual Taxes

### IBI (Impuesto sobre Bienes Inmuebles)
- Annual municipal property tax
- Based on valor catastral (cadastral value)
- Rates: 0.4-1.1% (urban), varies by municipality
- Paid annually, typically in September-November

### Basura (Waste Tax)
- Annual waste collection fee
- EUR 50-200/year depending on municipality

## Rental Income Tax

### Residents
- Rental income added to IRPF (progressive rates 19-47%)
- Deductible expenses: mortgage interest, repairs, insurance, community fees, IBI, depreciation
- 50% reduction for long-term residential rentals (contracts under LAU)

### Non-Residents (EU)
- 19% flat tax on net rental income (expenses deductible)

### Non-Residents (Non-EU)
- 24% flat tax on gross rental income (no deductions)

## Capital Gains Tax (at Sale)

### Residents
- Progressive rates on gain:
  - Up to EUR 6,000: 19%
  - EUR 6,000-50,000: 21%
  - EUR 50,000-200,000: 23%
  - Above EUR 200,000: 26%
- Primary residence: exempt if reinvested within 2 years
- Over-65 primary residence: always exempt

### Non-Residents
- 19% flat rate on the gain
- 3% of sale price withheld by buyer at signing (retención) and paid to Hacienda
  - Recoverable if actual tax liability is lower

### Plusvalía Municipal
- Municipal tax on land value increase
- Calculated based on cadastral land value and years of ownership
- Paid by seller (negotiable)

*Note: Spanish tax law varies by autonomous community. Always consult a fiscal advisor.*
