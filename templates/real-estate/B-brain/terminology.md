# Real Estate Terminology

## Portuguese Terms

| Term | Translation | Context |
|---|---|---|
| CPCV | Promissory Contract (Contrato-Promessa de Compra e Venda) | Binding purchase agreement before final deed |
| Escritura | Public Deed | Final property transfer deed signed at notary |
| IMI | Municipal Property Tax (Imposto Municipal sobre Imóveis) | Annual property tax based on patrimonial value |
| IMT | Property Transfer Tax (Imposto Municipal sobre Transmissões) | One-time tax paid at purchase |
| Caderneta Predial | Property Tax Document | Official property registration with tax authority |
| Certidão Permanente | Permanent Certificate | Land registry document proving ownership |
| Licença de Utilização | Usage License | Building permit certifying property's legal use |
| NIF | Tax Identification Number (Número de Identificação Fiscal) | Required for all property transactions |
| Finanças | Tax Authority (Autoridade Tributária) | Where property taxes are managed |
| Conservatória | Land Registry (Conservatória do Registo Predial) | Where property ownership is registered |
| Câmara Municipal | City Hall | Issues building permits and usage licenses |

## Italian Terms

| Term | Translation | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Compromesso | Preliminary Contract | Initial binding agreement (similar to CPCV) |
| Rogito | Notarial Deed | Final transfer deed signed before notaio |
| IMU | Municipal Property Tax (Imposta Municipale Unica) | Annual property tax (not on primary residence) |
| Catasto | Land Registry | Property cadaster with measurements and value |
| Agenzia delle Entrate | Revenue Agency | Tax authority for property transactions |
| Visura Catastale | Cadastral Survey | Official property data document |
| APE | Energy Performance Certificate | Required for sales and rentals |

## Spanish Terms

| Term | Translation | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Arras | Deposit Agreement | Earnest money contract before final sale |
| Escritura Pública | Public Deed | Final notarized purchase deed |
| IBI | Property Tax (Impuesto sobre Bienes Inmuebles) | Annual municipal property tax |
| ITP | Transfer Tax (Impuesto de Transmisiones Patrimoniales) | Tax on resale property purchases |
| Nota Simple | Registry Extract | Property ownership and encumbrance summary |
| Registro de la Propiedad | Property Registry | Official land and property registry |
| NIE | Foreigner ID Number (Número de Identidad de Extranjero) | Required for all transactions by non-residents |
| Plusvalía | Capital Gains Tax (municipal) | Municipal tax on land value increase at sale |
