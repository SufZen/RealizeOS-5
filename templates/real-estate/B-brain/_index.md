# Knowledge Base — Real Estate

This directory contains your venture's domain knowledge. Files here are automatically indexed and used as context in conversations.

## Structure

- **market-overview.md** — Your local market data and observations
- **terminology.md** — Real estate glossary for your market
- **portugal/** — Portuguese real estate process and regulations
- **italy/** — Italian real estate process and regulations
- **spain/** — Spanish real estate process and regulations

## Tips

- Keep market data current — update quarterly at minimum
- Add notes from client conversations to build institutional knowledge
- Document local process quirks that aren't in the official guides
- The more specific your knowledge base, the better your agents perform
