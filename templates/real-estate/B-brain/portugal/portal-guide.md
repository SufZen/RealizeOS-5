# Property Portal Guide — Portugal

## Idealista

**Market position:** Largest property portal in Iberia (PT + ES)
**Audience:** Both local and international buyers

### Listing Tips
- **Title:** Include typology + location + key feature (e.g., "T2 Setúbal, River View, Renovated")
- **Description length:** 500-1000 characters performs best
- **Photos:** Minimum 10, professional quality. First photo = exterior or best room
- **Price positioning:** Research competing listings in the same area before pricing
- **Boost visibility:** Respond to inquiries within 1 hour for algorithm priority
- **Language:** Portuguese primary, English secondary for international areas

## Imovirtual

**Market position:** Strong Portuguese presence, part of OLX group
**Audience:** Primarily Portuguese buyers

### Listing Tips
- **Description:** Detailed but scannable — use bullet points
- **Mandatory fields:** Fill ALL fields completely — incomplete listings rank lower
- **Photos:** At minimum 8, include floor plan if available
- **Featured listing:** Worth the investment for premium properties
- **Contact preference:** Phone calls are common — ensure contact number is active

## CasaSapo

**Market position:** Portuguese-focused portal
**Audience:** Local Portuguese market

### Listing Tips
- **Description:** Portuguese only, natural language
- **Updates:** Refresh listings regularly to maintain visibility
- **Certification:** Display AMI license number prominently

## General Best Practices

1. **Cross-post** to at least 2-3 portals for maximum reach
2. **Consistent pricing** across all portals — price discrepancies erode trust
3. **Update promptly** — remove sold properties, update price changes
4. **Track performance** — monitor views, inquiries, and time on market per portal
5. **Energy certificate** — mandatory for all listings (Certificado Energético)
6. **AMI license** — display your mediation license number on all listings
