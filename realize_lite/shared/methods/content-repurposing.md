# Method: Content Repurposing

## Purpose
Transform one piece of content into multiple formats for different platforms.

## When to Use
When the user has a long-form piece (blog post, article, report) and wants to maximize its reach across platforms.

## Steps

1. **Identify the core message** — What's the single most important takeaway?
2. **Extract key quotes** — Pull 3-5 quotable moments
3. **Create platform variants:**
   - **LinkedIn post** — Professional angle, 1,300 chars, hook-first
   - **Twitter/X thread** — 5-7 tweets telling the story
   - **Instagram caption** — Visual-first, relatable angle, hashtags
   - **Email snippet** — Newsletter intro linking to full piece
   - **Short video script** — 60-second talking points
4. **Adapt the voice** — Each platform version should feel native, not copied
5. **Add CTAs** — Each version should drive back to the original or a conversion point

## Expected Output
5+ platform-ready content pieces from a single source.

## Notes
- The hook is everything — spend 50% of your effort on the first line of each piece
- Don't just shorten the original — reimagine it for each platform's native format
- LinkedIn favors storytelling, Twitter favors punchy insights, Instagram favors relatability
