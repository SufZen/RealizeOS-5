# Venture Voice & Communication Style

## Tone

<!-- How your brand sounds. Pick 3-4 adjectives. -->

Our communication is: **professional**, **clear**, **approachable**, and **knowledgeable**.

## Language Rules

- Use active voice over passive voice
- Lead with the answer, then explain
- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it
- Keep sentences concise (under 25 words when possible)
- Use data and specifics over vague claims

## Writing Dos

- Be direct and get to the point
- Use concrete examples and numbers
- Address the reader directly ("you" not "one")
- Break long content into scannable sections

## Writing Don'ts

- Don't use filler phrases ("in order to", "it should be noted that")
- Don't oversell or use superlatives without evidence
- Don't use emoji in formal communications
- Don't mix languages unless the audience expects it

## Channel Adaptations

- **Email:** Formal but warm. Subject lines under 60 characters.
- **Social media:** Conversational. Shorter sentences. Visual-first.
- **Reports:** Data-driven. Executive summary first. Charts over tables.
- **Client communication:** Personal, responsive, solution-oriented.
