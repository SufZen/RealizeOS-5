# Knowledge Base

This directory is your venture's brain — domain knowledge, market intelligence, and reference material that your AI agents draw from when answering questions and creating content.

## How It Works

Files in this directory are automatically indexed and used as context when relevant to a conversation. The AI searches this knowledge base to ground its responses in your specific business reality.

## What to Add Here

- **domain-knowledge.md** — Your industry expertise, frameworks, and methodologies
- **market-notes.md** — Market observations, trends, competitor intel
- **client-profiles.md** — Ideal client descriptions, common pain points
- **process-guides.md** — Your standard operating procedures
- **reference-data.md** — Key benchmarks, pricing, statistics you frequently cite

## Tips

- Keep files focused on one topic each
- Use clear headings so the search index can find relevant sections
- Update regularly as your knowledge evolves
- Files can be `.md`, `.yaml`, or `.txt` format
